# OT Cyber Defence Quarterly Improvement Review Template
# ================================================================
# Description: Structured agenda and format for the quarterly
#   improvement review meeting. Ties together the maturity model,
#   continuous improvement cycle, and metrics framework.

#   to SOC leadership, OT Engineering, and executive sponsor.
#   The meeting should be 90–120 minutes. Schedule the next
#   review before closing the current one.
